# Customer Discovery Questions | Lab-3

## Project Title
Smart Assignment Tracker

## Target Respondent
University Students

## Consent and Context

1. May we ask you a few questions for our ICT105 course project?

2. Your participation is voluntary. We will not collect unnecessary personal data. Is it okay if we take notes?

## Recent Experience

3. Tell us about the last time you missed or almost missed an assignment deadline.

4. What course or assignment was involved?

5. What happened from start to finish?

## Current Workaround

6. How do you currently keep track of assignment deadlines?

7. What tools or methods do you use?
   - LMS
   - Calendar
   - Notes
   - Chat Groups
   - Reminders
   - Other

8. What is difficult, confusing, or frustrating about your current method?

## Pain Severity and Consequence

9. How often do you forget or almost forget assignment deadlines?

10. How serious is this problem on a scale of 1–5? Why?

11. What happens when you miss a deadline?

## Improvement Opportunities

12. What features would help you manage deadlines more effectively?

13. Would a dashboard that combines assignment deadlines in one place be useful to you? Why?

## Closing

14. Is there anything else we should understand about this problem?

15. May we use your anonymized response as part of our course project evidence?
